<objective>
Fix annual cooling and heating energy over-correction by removing thermal_mass_correction_factor entirely and eliminating double-correction bug.

Purpose: Verification identified that Plan 03-02's approach (thermal mass energy subtraction) caused massive over-correction (11.20 MWh vs [2.13, 3.67] MWh target for cooling). The root cause is **double-correction**: `thermal_mass_correction_factor` (0.20) reduces hvac_output_raw to 20%, then thermal_mass_energy_accounting tries to subtract thermal mass energy change from ALREADY-CORRECTED system. The physics reality is that `Ti_free` calculation already includes thermal mass effects via h_tr_em, h_tr_ms conductances and Cm capacitance. Therefore, NO multiplicative correction factor should be applied to hvac_output_raw.

Root Cause Analysis:
1. **Line 1947**: `hvac_output_energy = hvac_output_raw * thermal_mass_correction_factor` (0.20 for Case 900)
2. **Line 2076**: `if mass_energy_change > 0 { hvac_energy_for_step - mass_energy_change_cumulative_total }`
3. **Problem**: Thermal_mass_correction_factor and thermal_mass_energy_accounting are conflicting mechanisms
4. **Solution**: Remove thermal_mass_correction_factor entirely, use hvac_output_raw directly

Output: HVAC energy calculation using hvac_output_raw directly (no multiplicative factor), resulting in Case 900 annual cooling and heating energy within reference ranges.
</objective>
