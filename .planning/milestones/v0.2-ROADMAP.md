## Overview

This milestone delivered substantial ASHRAE 140 validation progress for the Fluxion building energy modeling engine. All 51 requirements across 7 phases are satisfied, with comprehensive diagnostics, performance optimization, and advanced analysis capabilities. However, **high-mass building annual energy predictions remain significantly outside ASHRAE tolerance bands** (229-322% error), representing a fundamental limitation of the ISO 13790 5R1C thermal network structure.

**Validation Status: Partial**
- ✅ Peak loads within reference ranges
- ✅ Solar radiation integration complete
- ✅ Free-floating temperatures within reference
- ❌ Annual energy for high-mass buildings exceeds reference by 229-322%
- ⚠️ Case 960 annual cooling fails (issue #273)

## Milestone Completion Notes

This milestone represents **partial validation** of the Fluxion BEM engine. Substantial progress has been made, but **critical gaps remain**:

### What Works
- Peak load prediction (heating and cooling within ±10% reference)
- Solar radiation integration (beam/diffuse, SHGC, incidence angles)
- Free-floating temperature validation
- Multi-zone inter-zone heat transfer (conductance, radiation, stack effect)
- All infrastructure (diagnostics, performance, CLI, testing)
- Research tools (sensitivity, delta, visualization, multi-reference)

### What Does NOT Work
- **Annual energy for high-mass buildings**: 229-322% error due to fundamental 5R1C model limitation
- Case 960 annual cooling: fails validation (issue #273)

### Why v0.2 (Not v1.0)
The 250%+ error in annual energy prediction makes the model **unsuitable for production building energy analysis** where annual energy accuracy is essential. While peak loads (critical for HVAC sizing) are accurate, the annual energy discrepancy indicates a fundamental physics modeling issue that requires either:
- Alternative thermal network structure (6R2C, 8R3C)
- Different integration approach
- Or acceptance that 5R1C is inherently limited for high-mass buildings

All planned work for this milestone is complete, and the gaps are thoroughly documented. The codebase is production-quality and fully tested. However, the validation status is **partial**, not complete.
